PUT YOUR PRODUCT PHOTOS IN THIS FOLDER
=======================================

1. Copy your product .jpg or .png photos here
2. Name them clearly, e.g:
   - desk-organizer.jpg
   - chopping-block.jpg
   - laptop-stand.jpg
   - spice-rack.jpg
   - catchall-tray.jpg
   - monitor-riser.jpg

3. Then open this file:
   src/data/products.js

4. Change the image line for each product to:
   image: '/products/your-filename.jpg',

TIPS FOR BEST RESULTS:
- Square photos (1:1) or portrait (4:5) look best on product cards
- Minimum 800x800 pixels for crisp display
- Good lighting, plain/wooden background preferred
- Compress at https://squoosh.app before uploading to keep size under 300KB
